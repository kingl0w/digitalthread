(a) THE MODEL

Two edges carry the whole design

A material defect travels the physical assembly tree. A design defect travels the revision lineage. Two defect classes, two traversal directions.

LOT-00049←MADE_FROM—S-000074 ENGINE/PRIMARY←COMPOSED_OF—S-000073 ENGINE
…←COMPOSED_OF—airframe 17265231—INSTALLED_IN→N478TW

The registry contributes real airframes and a real airframe-to-engine edge. Failure reports contribute real part serials. The supply-chain middle — lots, suppliers, work orders — is synthetic by design, with a ground-truth manifest, because no public source exposes it. That's what makes every number on this page checkable.
